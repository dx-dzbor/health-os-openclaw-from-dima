# Centenarian Decathlon Goals

> "The Centenarian Decathlon is a framework for backcasting your health goals.
> Instead of asking 'What can I do today?', ask 'What do I want to be capable of at 90,
> and what does that require today?'"
> — Peter Attia, MD

## Philosophy

The goal is not just to live to 90 — it's to be **functionally independent** at 90.
This means being able to perform everyday tasks that most 90-year-olds cannot.

**The 10-year rule:** Physical decline is predictable. What you can do at 80 will be ~30% less than at 70. Plan accordingly.

---

## My Centenarian Decathlon Events

### 1. Get up from the floor
**Requirement at 90:** Sit-to-stand without using hands
**Today's training:** Turkish get-ups, deep squats, floor-to-stand practice

### 2. Carry groceries up stairs
**Requirement at 90:** Farmer carry 20 lbs each hand, up one flight
**Today's training:** Loaded carries, step-ups, grip strength

### 3. Put luggage in overhead bin
**Requirement at 90:** Lift 15 lbs overhead
**Today's training:** Shoulder press, overhead stability work

### 4. Walk 3 miles in 60 minutes
**Requirement at 90:** 3 mph walking pace sustained
**Today's training:** Zone 2 cardio, daily walking habit

### 5. Hike with grandchildren
**Requirement at 90:** Uneven terrain, 2+ hours, some elevation
**Today's training:** Trail hiking, single-leg balance, ankle stability

### 6. Open jars and grip objects
**Requirement at 90:** >20 kg grip strength
**Today's training:** Dead hangs, farmer carries, grip work

### 7. Get in/out of bathtub safely
**Requirement at 90:** Single-leg balance, hip mobility
**Today's training:** Single-leg RDLs, hip mobility drills

### 8. React to prevent a fall
**Requirement at 90:** Quick reflexive movements
**Today's training:** Plyometrics, agility drills, balance training

### 9. Pick up a great-grandchild
**Requirement at 90:** Squat down, lift 30 lbs from floor
**Today's training:** Goblet squats, deadlifts, hip hinge pattern

### 10. Maintain cognitive sharpness
**Requirement at 90:** Conversation, decision-making, independence
**Today's training:** Zone 2 cardio (BDNF), omega-3, sleep optimization
